# Incident Investigation Intake Form

Fill in what you know. Leave blanks where you do not know yet; the skill will flag
gaps and ask you which assumption to proceed with rather than guessing. You do not
have to answer in order, and not every field applies to every incident. Plain
language is fine.

How to use: fill this out, then upload it and say "investigate this incident."
The skill will read your answers, size the investigation to the risk, and only ask
about the gaps.
